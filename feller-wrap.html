Types of Vinyl Wraps & Films
GLOSS VINYL WRAP
Anthracite | Hot Red | Storm Gray

Gloss Color wraps let you immediately restyle and customize the look and feel of just about anything you can think of. Originally designed for car wrapping, these vinyl films can be used on any hard, non-porous surface. Rated for both interior and exterior use, you love our selection of custom colors and our wide variety of sizes.

CARBON FIBER
4D | 3D | 3M™

Carbon Fiber vehicle wraps are probably one of the hottest, DIY vinyl wrap accessories on the market. They're versatile, (relatively) inexpensive, come in a variety of different colors, patterns and finishes and are made by a number of reputable brands. best of all, regardless of which brand and style of Carbon Fiber vinyl film you choose you can rest assured knowing that it has been engineered for wrapping.

MATTE VINYL WRAPS
Apple Green | Red Metallic | Orange

It used to be that you had no other options but now you can get the look of dips with the dependability and performance of vinyl films. At Rvinyl, we're always adding new and different vinyl films to our catalog but at present we're proud to car 3M™ 1080 Series films and Rwraps® vinyl wraps to help you get the ridiculously popular flat finish look for a price that's too good to refuse.

BRUSHED VINYL FILM
Aluminum | Titanium

Technically speaking, many of the brushed vinyl wrapping films we carry aren't called "brushed aluminum." ORACAL® prefers to call the individual colors in its 975BR Series just Brushed plus a color name. 3M™ and Avery™ do much the same thing but add the name of the metal so you get 1080 Series Brushed Steel or SW90 Brushed Bronze.

3M™ 1080 STAIN
Ocean Shimmer | Gold Dust Black

Once upon a time, the only time you'd see anything approaching a satin finish was when someone had primed their car or truck prior to painting it. Jump to the 21st Century and, like so many other things that just weren't cool, the satin-look is where it's at. At this point you may be saying, "Okay, I get that the satin"

SHADE SHIFTER VINYL WRAPS
Flip | Chameleon

Rwraps® Chameleon shade-shifter wraps are a truly unique vinyl film that let you quickly and easily upgrade, update and restyle the look of your car, truck or SUV. And, even though they were originally developed for automotive use, they can be used on any hard and non-porous surface so our customers have wrapped everything from tabletops to speedboats.
